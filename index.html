About Me

I love my family, I love my country and I love our people. But I always figured there is more for me outside of Rohan. First, I figured, that I was in love with the throne heir of Gondor, Aragorn, but that was not it. My uncle always wanted to protect me and so I chose to go with him to war. Not just because I know how to use a sword, but also to fight for my beloved ones and to protect them.

And then, there was the fight. And of course, it was so scary and the masses of orcs and other enemies were intimitating. But I took a deep breath and went with it. I fought for my life and the lives of my family. I even defeated an olifant. But then... there was the witch king of Angmar. A horrifying bastard. And he was about to kill my uncle! So I gathered all of my courage and stood between the bastard and my uncle.

Of course I married someone I loved and of course I stayed true to myself. But I gave up the dream of glory in battle and devoted my life to peace. But sometimes, I wish for more.
And now I outlast the others, I live on. In another time. I am strong and I choose to do what knowone expects from me, I learn to code. And I will be good. Because if I want to, I can do everything. So can you.
